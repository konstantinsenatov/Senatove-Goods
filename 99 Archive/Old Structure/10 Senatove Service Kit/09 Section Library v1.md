# Section Library v1

## Selection Principles
- Keep v1 focused and production-ready.
- Cover essential service-site storytelling and conversion flows.
- Prefer fewer, high-quality sections over broad but shallow coverage.

## Banners
- **Service Hero**: headline, subheadline, primary CTA, secondary CTA, optional trust note.
- **Split Hero with Media**: text block + image/video, two balanced variants.

## TextBlocks and Media
- **Editorial Intro Block**: section heading, lead paragraph, body text, optional kicker label.
- **Two-Column Text + Media**: narrative text with supporting image, left/right media variants.
- **Process Steps Block**: short step list (3-6 items) for service methodology explanation.

## Cards
- **Service Cards Grid**: feature/service cards with title, short description, optional icon.
- **Value Pillars Cards**: concise positioning pillars (quality, speed, expertise, etc.).

## FAQs
- **Standard FAQ Accordion**: question/answer repeater with optional grouped heading.

## Testimonials
- **Quote Testimonials**: client quote, name, role/company, optional avatar.
- **Case Highlight Testimonial**: testimonial plus short impact metric or outcome line.

## CTA / Contact
- **Conversion CTA Band**: concise CTA strip with headline + action.
- **Contact Detail Block**: contact channels, short form intro copy, business availability note.

## v1 Notes
This library is intentionally constrained to support premium service-site launches quickly while preserving visual consistency and editorial clarity.
